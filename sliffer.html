How to install shortcuts using Sliffer?
  1. Browse or Search: Open the Sliffer app and browse through the available shortcuts or use the search functionality to find specific ones.
  2. Select Shortcut: Once you've found a shortcut you want to install, tap on it to view more details.
  3. Install: Look for a "GET" button within the shortcut's details. Tap on it to begin the installation process.
  4. Confirmation: Sliffer may prompt you to confirm the installation. Confirm your choice to proceed.
  5. Completion: Once the installation is complete, the shortcut should be available for use on your iOS device.
  6. Enjoy: Start using the installed shortcut to streamline tasks, enhance productivity, or add new features to your device.
